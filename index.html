Dental Implants

BLANCO DENTAL - Amici del Sorriso

Dental implants are replacement tooth roots. Implants provide a strong foundation for fixed (permanent) or removable replacement teeth that are made to match your natural teeth.

  • Patient: In most cases, anyone healthy enough to undergo a routine dental extraction or oral surgery can be considered for a dental implant.
  • Success Rates: Success rates of dental implants vary, depending on where in the jaw the implants are placed but, in general, dental implants have a success rate of up to 98%. With proper care (see below), implants can last a lifetime.

Dental Stemm Cells

BLANCO DENTAL - Amici del Sorriso

Dental Stemm Cells can be used to treat various spine and orthopedic conditions, cardiovascular diseases and may also be useful in plastic surgery.They can also be used to treat neuronal degenerative disorders such as Parkinson's disease, cerebral palsy, Alzheimer's disease, and other such disorders.

  • Patients: Children between 0-12 years old as well as adults under 30 years of age if teeth are being extracted for orthodontic purposes.
  • Benefits: Dental Stem Cells may also be useful for close relatives of the donor such as grandparents, parents and siblings. Apart from these, Dental Stemm Cells banking is more economical when compared to cord blood and may be complementary to cord cell banking.
